Efficiency Calculation Methods

SEER Calculation

SEER = (Total BTU Cooling / Total Watt-Hours) × 3412

Weighted average over three temperature bins: 95°F, 82°F, and 67°F test conditions.

AFUE Calculation

AFUE = (Annual Output BTU / Annual Input BTU) × 100

Measured over full heating season with varying outdoor temperatures.

HSPF Calculation

HSPF = (Total BTU Heating / Total Watt-Hours) × 3412

Weighted average over seven regional temperature bins.

Performance Optimization Strategies

Load Calculations

Manual J calculations for accurate capacity sizing. Oversizing penalties: 3-5% efficiency loss per 10% oversizing.

Airflow Optimization

Target velocities: supply ducts 800-1200 FPM, return ducts 600-800 FPM. Pressure drops <0.1" w.c. for optimal efficiency.

Refrigerant Management

Proper charge: ±5% tolerance. Superheat: 10-15°F evaporator, 5-10°F condenser for optimal COP.

Data-Driven Efficiency Monitoring

Implement continuous monitoring with kWh meters, temperature sensors, and data logging. Trend analysis for predictive maintenance and efficiency optimization.
