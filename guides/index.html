Safety and Technical Considerations

All procedures require adherence to electrical safety standards (NEC 2020), refrigerant handling protocols (EPA Section 608), and general HVAC best practices. Use calibrated instruments and document all measurements for quality assurance.

Tools and Equipment Requirements

Professional-grade tools are essential for accurate diagnostics and repairs. Recommended equipment includes digital multimeters, pressure gauges, airflow meters, and data logging devices for comprehensive system analysis.
