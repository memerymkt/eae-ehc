HVAC Safety Checklist Printable

Regular safety inspections prevent accidents and ensure your HVAC system operates safely. Use this printable checklist to inspect your furnace, air conditioner, and ductwork before each heating and cooling season.

Photo by Joe Dudeck on Unsplash - Safety first

General Safety Checklist

  • Test carbon monoxide detectors monthly
  • Check smoke detectors and replace batteries
  • Ensure proper clearance around outdoor units
  • Verify electrical panels are accessible
  • Check for gas leaks with soapy water test
  • Inspect for visible damage or corrosion

Furnace Safety Checks

  • Inspect heat exchanger for cracks or damage
  • Check burner flames are steady blue
  • Verify proper gas pressure
  • Test limit switches and safety controls
  • Inspect venting for blockages or leaks
  • Check thermostat operation and calibration

Air Conditioner Safety

  • Check refrigerant lines for damage
  • Inspect electrical connections
  • Verify proper air flow through coils
  • Test condensate drain function
  • Check outdoor unit mounting and stability
  • Inspect fan blades for damage

Ductwork and Vents

  • Check for disconnected or damaged ducts
  • Inspect registers for proper function
  • Verify no obstructions in ductwork
  • Check for excessive dust accumulation
  • Inspect flexible ducts for tears
  • Verify return air grilles are clear

Electrical Safety

  • Test circuit breakers and fuses
  • Check wiring for damage or overheating
  • Verify ground fault protection
  • Inspect capacitors and contactors
  • Test control board operation
  • Check for proper voltage at components

Emergency Preparedness

  • Know location of emergency shut-off valves
  • Have emergency contact numbers posted
  • Keep fire extinguisher nearby
  • Understand system shutdown procedures
  • Prepare emergency repair kit
  • Have backup heating plan for winter

Maintenance Schedule

Perform safety checks:

  • Monthly: Basic visual inspections
  • Quarterly: Test safety controls
  • Semi-annually: Professional tune-ups
  • Annually: Comprehensive safety inspection
