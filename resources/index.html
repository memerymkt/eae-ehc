Engineering Reference Data

Material Properties

  • Copper Tubing: K = 0.34 BTU/hr·ft·°F, thermal conductivity specifications
  • Insulation R-Values: Fiberglass R-3.5/in, foam R-4.5/in, reflective barriers
  • Duct Materials: Galvanized steel 0.05" thickness, flex duct pressure ratings

Design Parameters

  • Air Velocity: Supply 600-900 FPM, return 400-600 FPM
  • Pressure Drop: Ductwork <0.1" w.c./100ft, filters <0.3" w.c.
  • Temperature Rise: Furnaces 50-70°F, heat pumps 30-50°F
  • Humidity Control: 40-60% RH optimal, dehumidification 50-70 pints/day
