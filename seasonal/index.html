Technical Maintenance Specifications

Filter Specifications

  • Winter: MERV 8-11 for dust and pollen filtration
  • Summer: MERV 8-13 for humidity and allergen control
  • Pressure Drop: <0.3" w.c. maximum for optimal airflow

Refrigerant Management

Seasonal charge verification: ±5% tolerance. Superheat measurements: winter 8-12°F, summer 10-15°F.

Electrical System Checks

Voltage stability: 240V ±5% for compressors. Ground resistance: <1 ohm. Capacitor testing: within 10% of rated capacitance.

Climate-Specific Considerations

Portland's marine climate requires enhanced moisture protection. Implement desiccant driers in humid periods and monitor condensation drainage systems.

Preventive Maintenance Schedule

  • Monthly: Filter inspection and cleaning
  • Quarterly: Thermostat calibration and sensor verification
  • Semi-Annually: Refrigerant charge and electrical system testing
  • Annually: Complete system inspection and efficiency testing
